Basic Example Select a drawing mode and use the mouse to draw shapes on the map. To stop drawing either press the selected drawing mode button again to toggle the mode off, select a different drawing mode, or press ESC. To delete shapes press the eraser button and then click on a shape to delete it. To edit a shape press the pencil button and then either select a shape to see the points that can be editted, or simply click press and drag pushpins. Also, when a shape is selected you can also change it's color and line styles.
